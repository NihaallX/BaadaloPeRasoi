Baadalo Pe Rasoi
 Logo Usage Guidelines & Brand Protection Manual
 
A playful and comforting cloud kitchen identity inspired by warmth, homemade
 food, and dreamy visual storytelling.
 
Brand Personality
The Baadalo Pe Rasoi identity is soft, approachable, playful, and emotionally warm. The rounded
typography and cloud symbol represent comfort food, imagination, and a welcoming homemade
experience. These guidelines help maintain consistency across websites, packaging, social media, and
print applications.
■ Logo Do’s

Maintain Clear Space
Always leave enough breathing space around the logo. Minimum spacing should equal the cloud icon
height.
Use Approved Colors
Use the original berry pink, white, or monochrome variations only.
Use Vector Formats
Use SVG or vector files for websites and packaging to maintain sharpness.
Scale Proportionally
Resize the logo uniformly from corners without distortion.
Use Clean Backgrounds
Prefer soft creams, beige, light pinks, or dark solid backgrounds for strong readability.
Use the Cloud Symbol Consistently
The cloud icon may be used separately for favicons, stickers, and social media icons.
Ensure Readability
Keep the logo large enough for mobile visibility and digital clarity.
■ Logo Don’ts
Do Not Stretch the Logo
Avoid squishing or stretching the logo horizontally or vertically.
Do Not Change the Typography
Do not replace the hand-drawn playful lettering with unrelated fonts.
Avoid Heavy Effects
Do not use shadows, bevels, glows, gradients, or unnecessary outlines.
Avoid Busy Backgrounds
Never place the logo directly over cluttered photography or patterns without contrast.
Do Not Modify the Cloud Shape
The cloud symbol should remain simple and recognizable.
Avoid Low Contrast Usage
Do not use colors that reduce readability or visual impact.

Do Not Overcrowd
Avoid adding too many supporting illustrations around the logo.
Website Usage Recommendations
 Recommended website logo width: 200–300 px
 Export SVG for responsive crisp quality
 Use transparent PNGs for overlays
 Use the cloud icon as favicon or app icon
 Recommended artboard size: 800 × 800 px
